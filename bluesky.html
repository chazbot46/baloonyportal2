Bluesky Domain Linking

Learn how to link your Bluesky account to a custom domain using the Baloony Domain Management Center.

Link your Bluesky Account to a Domain

This guide walks you through creating a domain and linking it to your Bluesky account so it becomes your Bluesky handle.

Step 1: Access the Domain Management Center

Open the Domain Management Center .

Log in with your credentials. If you don't have them yet, request access here.

Step 2: Create your domain

Click Create Domain.

Enter the handle you would like and submit the request. Then wait for it to register.

Step 3: Link the domain to Bluesky

Once the domain shows Active, click Manage DNS.

Select the request type Link to Bluesky.

Step 4: Enter your Bluesky DID

Enter your Bluesky DID value.

If you don't know how to find it, click here.

Press submit. Processing may take a few minutes. Once complete you will receive a confirmation email.

Step 5: Set your custom Bluesky handle

  1. Open Bluesky.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Account.
  4. Open Handle.
  5. Enter your domain handle (example: yourname.baloony.xyz).
  6. Confirm the change.

Bluesky will automatically verify the domain using the DNS configuration you created earlier.

How to find your Bluesky DID

You can easily find your DID directly inside Bluesky:

  1. Open Bluesky.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Account.
  4. Open Handle.
  5. Click Use Custom Domain.

Your DID will be displayed there and will look like: 

did:plc:xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Copy that value and paste it into the Domain Management Center request form.
