Bluesky Domain Linking

Learn how to link your Bluesky account to a custom domain using the Baloony Domain Management Center.

Link your Bluesky Account to a Domain

This guide will walk you through creating a domain and linking it to your Bluesky account so it becomes your Bluesky handle.

Step 1: Access the Domain Management Center

Open the Domain Management Center .

Log in with your credentials. If you don't have them yet, request access here.

Step 2: Create your domain

Click "Create Domain".

Type the handle you would like and submit the request.

Wait for the system to register your domain.

Step 3: Link the domain to Bluesky

Once the domain shows "Active", click "Manage DNS".

Select the request type "Link to Bluesky".

Step 4: Enter your Bluesky DID

Enter your Bluesky DID value.

If you don't know how to find it, click here.

After submitting the request, it may take a few minutes to process. Once finished, you will receive a confirmation email.

Step 5: Change your Bluesky handle

Once your domain has been successfully linked, you can set it as your Bluesky handle.

  1. Open Bluesky.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Account.
  4. Click Handle.
  5. Enter your new domain handle (example: yourname.baloony.xyz).
  6. Confirm the change.

Bluesky will verify the domain automatically using the DNS configuration you created earlier.

How to find your Bluesky DID

  1. Open Bluesky in your browser.
  2. Open your profile page.
  3. Open the browser developer tools (usually F12).
  4. Go to the Network tab.
  5. Reload the page.
  6. Look for a request containing your profile data.
  7. Your DID will look like this:
did:plc:xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Copy this value and paste it into the Domain Management Center request form.
